  • Popocatepetl

    Surreal adventure bike and hike day. We started from the van down low to access the Iztaccihuatl trailhead. Popocatepetl or Popo provided a breathtaking backdrop with its continuous eruptions.

    Popo was on the front page of the Wall Street Journal 4 days after we summited Izta. The park that we stayed at was closed and a couple of nearby towns were evacuated. In the end Popo did not go off and is still quite active to this day.
